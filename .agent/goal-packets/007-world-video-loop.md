Status: active

Mission: Repeatedly improve WorldFactory by generating distinct 15-second vertical place videos, reviewing each result, and carrying one evidence-backed issue through the next repair.

Requirements:

- Review the active goal workspace, current video, contact sheet, manifest, and issue graph.
- Select either the highest-impact open issue or the next distinct queued place.
- Preserve the prompt and seed for repairs, change one factory variable, and regenerate through the local world-video lane.
- Validate engine proof, media integrity, motion, portrait framing, opening clarity, and cross-biome readability.
- Record each review, issue, change, evidence link, resolution, and next action in the graph.
- Retain one full current review video, compact prior evidence, and no upload or publication permission.
- Continue until input or approval is required, a real blocker exists, or the current iteration is reviewable with its next issue recorded.

Final Output:

The reviewer opens one current 15-second vertical video, immediately sees a distinct generated place, follows its build and validation, sees the completed world again, and can trace every accepted or rejected improvement through compact evidence and graph history.
